Luxury Pool

The ideal spot to enjoy fresh juices and light meals - the perfect complement to a day by the pool, or simply to take a relaxing break.

Enjoy your time by the 18 meter long swimming pool reflecting azure blue water and spectacular sunsets along with an exclusive pool bar.
The poolside is ideal for relaxation, de stress, and a fitness regime. Attached is a baby pool for kids and families to keep their little ones engaged.

The Pool Bar offers the signature cocktails along with a continental menu served at the sun deck.
